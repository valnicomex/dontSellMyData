dontSellMyData

dontSellMyData is an activist toolkit project made for the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program. With the help of them and a little bit of Google searches, I, valnicomex (aka val), created this page to spread awareness on digital privacy. Hopefully this toolkit helped you learn something and become more digitally aware. :)
