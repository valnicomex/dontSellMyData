support the cause

If you haven't filled out the petition yet, please do so. We are asking that companies around the world implement some kind of standard to help give users more freedom of how they want their data to be collected and/ or used. For example:

  • Have alternate options for privacy policies when registering or having the option to change it in settings.

  • I agree to the Privacy Policy Variant 1 (I approve for INSERT COMPANY NAME HERE to collect my data and sell it to third-party companies)

    I agree to the Privacy Policy Variant 2 (I approve for INSERT COMPANY NAME HERE to collect my data but not sell it to third-party companies)

    I agree to the Privacy Policy Variant 3 (I approve for INSERT COMPANY NAME HERE to to not collect my data therefore not having any data to use for the company and not having any data to sell to third party companies)

  • Comply with CCPA standard to have an option to stop a company from selling your data.

  • Have an option to determine what types of data you are willing to have collected.
